## Reading the records

Each atomic row explicitly records all mandatory fields. In the compact columns, labels are retained rather than implied: `Test`, `Runtime` and `Config` are separate evidence fields; `Reach`, `Public` and `Offer` are separate availability fields; and `Verified`, `Freshness` and `Trigger` are separate governance fields. `None observed` means the audit found no evidence at the audited commit; it is not evidence that a capability cannot exist in a deployment.

Technical maturity is one of `static implementation`, `test-backed`, `runtime-contract verified`, `operationally evidenced` or `not evidenced`. Commercial truth is one of `delivered`, `qualified`, `approved-next`, `exploratory` or `prohibited`. No row is approved for sale merely because it is reachable.
