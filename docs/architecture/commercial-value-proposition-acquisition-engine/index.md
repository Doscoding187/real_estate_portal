## Approved baseline

AAE is not a collection of Advertise With Us pages. It is the authority that connects:

Platform strategy → audience problem → value proposition → product capability → commercial offer → public experience → conversion journey → onboarding → product fulfilment.

The foundational platform value model is:

**Discovery → Trust → Operations → Progression**

Approved in principle:

- a central business-acquisition hub with distinct audience journeys;
- the conceptual separation of for-business, partners and narrower advertise products;
- outcome-led, product-truthful commercial communication;
- structured and version-controlled commercial content authority before any database CMS; and
- banks and bond originators should default to a strategic-partnership, referral or integration motion; any ordinary advertiser or subscription proposition requires explicit validation and an AAE authority amendment.

Unresolved:

- final routes and SEO treatment;
- final pricing, offers, entitlement authority and public-price rules;
- agency product versus agent-volume-tier model;
- service-provider commercial model;
- exact developer sales and campaign model; and
- AAE-S1 capability truth, offer and onboarding contracts.

## Mandatory review rule

Before planning or implementing changes affecting public commercial messaging, stakeholder journeys, pricing presentation, plans and entitlements, onboarding, marketing content, partner acquisition, sales collateral or audience-specific product propositions, review the current Commercial Value Proposition & Acquisition Engine authority and record whether the proposed work aligns with it, extends it or requires an explicit authority amendment.

## Future AAE-slice compliance checklist

Every future AAE audit, decision record or implementation plan must answer:

1. Which approved audience does this work serve?
2. Which customer problem and desired outcome does it address?
3. Which part of Discovery, Trust, Operations or Progression does it support?
4. Which real platform capabilities support the public promise?
5. Does it introduce or repeat any unverified claim?
6. What is the commercial motion: self-service, assisted sale, campaign or strategic partnership?
7. Does it affect pricing, entitlements, offers or eligibility?
8. Does it require an amendment to the governing authority?
9. Which authority document and section were reviewed?
10. How will the public promise remain consistent with the product experience?

## Amendment and supersession rules

- AAE-S0 is the initial governing baseline. Supporting research informs it but does not automatically change it.
- Future discoveries may align with, extend, amend or supersede the baseline; each new document must state which relationship applies.
- A material change to the audience structure, master value proposition, commercial motion, pricing architecture or content authority requires an explicit amendment or superseding decision record.
- Superseded documents remain in Git history and are marked clearly; they are not silently overwritten.
- Implementation decisions must not be presented as approved product strategy unless the relevant authority has been updated.

## Boundaries

This authority does not authorise application code, database schema, CMS tables, routes, UI, pricing logic, plan definitions or public pages. AAE-S1 must be reviewed and approved before AAE-S2 launch implementation may begin.
