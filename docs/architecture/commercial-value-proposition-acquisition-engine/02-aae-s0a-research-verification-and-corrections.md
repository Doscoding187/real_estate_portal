## Corrections and qualifications

### Property24 professional-pricing transparency

**Correction to 01:** The statement that Property24 professional pricing is not public is incomplete.

Property24 exposes public product-overview material, Small Independent Business and Rental Package material, eligibility information, and a detailed 2026 rate card for certain subscriptions and value-added products. New-customer qualification and commercial setup may still involve a relationship manager.

**Governing interpretation:** This is **hybrid pricing transparency**, not fully hidden negotiated pricing and not fully self-service purchasing.

**Evidence class:** Official current source for the rate-card and eligibility materials; architectural inference for the hybrid classification.

### Public rate card is not self-service purchase

Published rates or eligibility information do not establish that a customer can select, pay for and activate a product without commercial qualification or assisted setup.

Future AAE work must record separately:

- pricing visibility;
- eligibility transparency;
- purchase and payment path;
- activation path; and
- sales-assistance requirement.

**Evidence class:** Architectural interpretation informed by the Property24 correction and the reviewed competitor patterns.

### Competition Commission implications

The South African Competition Commission Online Intermediation Platforms Market Inquiry is regulatory evidence relevant to commercial architecture. The research identified remedies and concerns relating to interoperability, incoming-listing charges, long-term contracts, differential treatment of smaller agencies and disclosure of paid placement.

Future AAE work must therefore make eligibility, plan limits, contract period, cancellation, feed and syndication terms, small-business treatment and promoted-placement disclosure explicit design and commercial-governance concerns.

This does not prescribe Property Listify pricing or create a direct legal conclusion for every product.

**Evidence class:** Regulatory source for the inquiry and remedies; architectural inference for the resulting design principles.

### Banks and bond originators

The research supports treating banks and originators as a likely partnership, referral or integration motion in the sampled market evidence. It does **not** establish a universal rule that lenders can never be advertisers or that every future relationship belongs outside paid placement.

**Governing interpretation:** Treat finance as a strategic-partnership hypothesis pending commercial, legal, product and demand evidence. Do not expose it as a normal subscription proposition without an explicit AAE amendment.

**Evidence class:** Official and public partnership evidence where documented; architectural inference for the commercial-motion recommendation.

### NoBrokerHood comparison

NoBrokerHood is evidence that a property company can operate an adjacent, separately positioned product for a different buyer. It is not a direct analogue for a service-provider marketplace in which plumbers, electricians, inspectors or similar providers buy leads, territory access or promoted placement.

**Governing interpretation:** It supports product and motion separation only. It does not settle Service Listify monetisation.

**Evidence class:** Official product-positioning evidence; architectural inference.

### Scope of universal claims

Statements such as “no portal publishes professional pricing” or “no portal offers a public lender-partner page” must not be used as universal market facts.

The supported formulation is:

> Within the reviewed sample, comprehensive instantly purchasable professional pricing was uncommon. Competitors used a mixture of public rate cards, indicative product information, account-specific pricing and sales-assisted qualification.

And:

> Within the reviewed sample, lending relationships were primarily embedded, referral-based or strategically negotiated rather than sold as ordinary advertising subscriptions.

**Evidence class:** Sample-bounded research interpretation.
